SPELL GUIDE (1/2)

To learn each spell you need to have a certain minimum amount of class levels.
Every hero starts at class level 1 and receives an extra class level when upgrading in a class.
You can cast as many spells as you have class levels before resting.

Caster level 1
  Cure light wounds: cures 5 hit points. 
  Doom: shakens an enemy.
  Inflict light wounds: causes 5 points of damage.
  
Caster level 3
  Bear's endurance: +2 constitution bonus.
  Bull's strength: +2 strength bonus. 
  Cat's grace: +2 dexterity bonus.
  Cure moderate wounds: cures 13 hit points.
  Eagle's splendor: +2 charisma bonus.
  Fox's cunning: +2 intelligence bonus.
  Inflict moderate wounds: causes 13 points of damage.
  Owl's wisdom: +2 wisdom bonus.
  Summon Dretch: summons a Dretch to your team.
